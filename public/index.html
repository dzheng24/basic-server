About Me

My name is David Zheng. I am a software developer at Code Fellows. I enlisted in the Navy after high school as an avionics technician in a F/A-18 squadron. I spent four years in the military. My background is in sports medicine; I received my bachelor’s degree in athletic training from Michigan State University. Prior to Code Fellows, I worked at a Boeing manufacturing plant as an athletic trainer. I decided to pursue software development because I quickly realized being an athletic trainer wasn’t my calling, and I needed to challenge myself. My interest for coding grew from my love of learning different spoken languages. Mandarin is my native language, and I’m currently learning Spanish and Japanese. I believe in empathy, and kindness; my past experiences as an athletic trainer can shape me into a valuable member in the tech industry because of my passion for helping people.
